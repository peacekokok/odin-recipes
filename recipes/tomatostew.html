Tomato Stew

I have a chapati recipe and I said chapati goes well with stew. Thus, I will be teaching how to make stew

Description

A stew is a liquid formed by mixing and grinding multiple ingridients into a texture that resembles a paste

Tomato stew is quick and easy to make when you are in a rush and has the following ingredents

If you experience any texture issues this dish might not be for you.

Ingredients

  • 3 large tomatoes
  • 2 red onions
  • 2 tablespoons of ginger
  • 2-3 scotch bonnet peppers(depending on your spice tolerence)
    • Can substitute habanero
  • 2 Red Bell Peppers
    • Can substitute Ajvar
  • tomato paste
  • 4 table spoons Olive oil
  • 2 table spoons Palm oil
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • black pepper
  • Cayenne pepper
  • salt
  • maggi cube
  • garlic powder

Steps

  1. Cut the garlic into small pieces
  2. Cut the onion nto small pieces
  3. Cut the red bell and scotch bonnet peppers
  4. Section out the ginger
  5. Blend 3 tomotoes, one onion, ginger, scotch bonnet, bell peppers and the garlic until smooth
  6. After blending set aside the mixture and heat up a non stick pan on stove
  7. Add olive oil
  8. Wait for olive oil to heat up
  9. After olive oil is heated add palm oil
  10. After palm oil is heated add onions
  11. Wait for onions to turn a golden color
  12. Add garlic powder, maggi cube, a pinch of salt, cayenne pepper, black pepper on onion
  13. After the onions have absorbed the spices add a tablespoon of tomato paste
  14. Add the tomato mixture from earlier
  15. Use a wooden spoon to mix until the stew starts to bubble
  16. After bubbling take off the heat

Takeaways

Serve with

chapati

, rice, bread or by itself. The stew is great especially if someone is sick.
